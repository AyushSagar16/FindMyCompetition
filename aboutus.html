About Us

Our story

Finding competitions is hard. Many students like us went through the struggle so we decided to make it our mission to provide an accessible and convenient list of all competitions near you. We’ve gone through the problem many times and decided, there needs be a solution for this. That’s why we created FindMyCompetition, an easy, accessible, and fast way to find competitions.
