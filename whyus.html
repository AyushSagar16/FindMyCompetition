Why Us?

We help students find competitions that interests them!

Academics

Competitions are a great way students to improve their academic performance and they are a great way for them to improve their skills. 

Communications

While competing in competitions that they found on FindMyCompetition they will meet kids their age and improve their communication skills. 

Hard Work

While studying for these competitions students will learn to respect hard work which is a skill that will help them tremendously for the rest of their lives. 

College Applications

College applications look for students who constantly challenge themselves and what better way to do that then to join competitions which you can easily find on FindMyCompetition.
